About Virtual photography

Virtual photography, often referred to as "in-game photography", is the art of capturing images within virtual environments created by video games. It involves using the in-game camera tools provided by the game developers or sometimes third-party modifications to compose and capture images that showcase the game's graphics, artistry, atmosphere, and storytelling elements.

Virtual photographers utilize various techniques such as adjusting camera angles, depth of field, lighting, and filters to create stunning and visually compelling images. They often approach virtual photography with the same artistic sensibilities as traditional photography, focusing on composition, framing, and capturing the essence of the virtual world they are exploring.
