About sotojeriaaugusto

I'm a 21 year old Full Stack student with an insatiable curiosity for coding and exploration. When I'm not buried with studies, you'll find me exploring the intricacies of programming languages like C++ and delving into reverse engineering and game hacking.

My current focus lies in leveraging my skills to enhance virtual photography experiences within games. Through reverse engineering and manipulation of game mechanics, I develop tools and utilities in C++ to enable features such as free-roaming camera controls and dynamic lighting adjustments.

Having recently completed the first unit of my Full Stack program, I'm equipped with a solid foundation in web development technologies and eager to apply my expertise to new and challenging projects in both frontend and backend development.
